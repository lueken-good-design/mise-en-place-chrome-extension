# Mise en Place Recipe Importer - Chrome Extension

## Short Description (132 characters max for Chrome Web Store)
Import recipes from any website directly to your Mise en Place account. Quick import or bulk import multiple recipes at once.

## Detailed Description

**Save recipes from anywhere on the web to your Mise en Place recipe book with one click.**

The Mise en Place Recipe Importer makes it effortless to build your digital recipe collection. Whether you're browsing food blogs, recipe sites, or YouTube cooking videos, you can import recipes directly to your account without copying and pasting.
